About

M&M Artshop was founded by Mr. Charles Barandoc in October 23, 2021, M&M Artshop is an online based store. Way back 2021 I started selling a hand painted tote bag, where I asked the customer what design they like and I paint it on tote bag or a canvas bag. I got a decent sale for a start up business, and one day I tell to my partner that way back in my elementary days, I learned to crochet in my subject "Edukasyong Pantahanan at Pangkabuhayan" or EPP. I create a crochet earmuffs and send it to my partner and she said that I should sell it to my shop. I added crochet products in my shop, the first crochet product I sell was crochet accessories. Then later on I started to sell crochet flowers, and got big sale by selling crochet products.

Our Mission, Crafting joy through crochet, we create high-quality handmade products to inspire creativity and connection in our community.

Our Vision, To be a beloved destination for crochet enthusiasts, celebrating the beauty of handmade craftsmanship while fostering creativity and sustainability.
